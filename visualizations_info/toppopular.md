### Popularity Plot
This bar chart shows the **Top 50** most popular items in the training dataset, ranked by their total number of interactions (e.g., sum of ratings, or count of clicks).

* **What it shows:** The global popularity distribution of your items. This is the "model" learned by the Top Popular algorithm.
* **What to look for:** The recommendations from this model will *only* consist of items from the top of this list. It is a non-personalized baseline that is heavily biased towards these popular items.
